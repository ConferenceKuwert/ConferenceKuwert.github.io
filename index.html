Geometric PDEs in Freiburg A conference in honor of the 60th Birthday of Ernst Kuwert
Nov 29 to Dec 03, 2021
Albert-Ludwigs-Universität, Freiburg im Breisgau
Home Registration Program Directions Participants

Welcome to the webpage of the conference that is organized to honor Ernst Kuwert and his mathematics.
We expect this conference to take place as an event on-site. Please register if you would like to attend this conference.

Registration

The registration is now open. Registration is possible until October 20th, 2021. In order to register, please write an Email to
conferenceKuwert@gmail.com
containing the following information:

  • - your name,
  • - your university and department.
Due to Corona restrictions a registration is necessary if you would like to attend the conference in person.

Financial support for young participants

PhD students, PostDocs and other young researchers can apply for financial support for travel and accommodation. Please send a CV and a short statement explaining how you would benefit from the conference to
conferenceKuwert@gmail.com
The application deadline for financial support is October 1st, 2021.
