Financial support for young participants

PhD students, PostDocs and other young researchers can apply for financial support for travel and accommodation. Please send a CV and a short statement explainig how you would benefit from the conference to
conferenceKuwert@gmail.com
The application deadline for financial support is October 1st, 2021.
